About Me

I was born and raised in Los Angeles, California. I have been married for 14 years and have two rapidly growing kids. I joined the US Navy in September 1999 in the Advanced Electronic Computer Field and chose the rate of Fire Controlman. Some of my duty stations include USS Thorn (DD-988), USS Cape St. George (CG-71), USS Enterprise (CVN 65), USS Winston S. Churchill (DDG-81). I will be retiring after 20 years and 4 months in January 2020.

Favorite Things

Most of my favorite things revolve around church and sports. I teach both a Men's Bible Study, and 5th grade Sunday School at my church. My love for sports starts with the LA Dodgers since I was 7 years old. My favorite football team is due to LA not having a team and my uncle always sending me jerseys- Pittsburgh Steelers. I love to play basketball, baseball, or any competitive sport.
